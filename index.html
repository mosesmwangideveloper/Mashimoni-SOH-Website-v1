What We Build In Mathare

Here's what we're building in Mathare to empower the community and create lasting change.

Evening Library & Learning Hub

A safe, welcoming space where kids and teens read, learn, and grow — with books, resources, and quiet study space.

Group Discussions

Real conversations. Bold opinions. Critical thinking. We give every young person a voice — and the courage to use it.

E-Learning Sessions

One screen. World-class educators. Right here in Mathare. Learning has no borders when curiosity leads the way.

Chess Training & Competitions

Strategy. Patience. One move at a time. Chess teaches us as youth to think, plan, and compete — fearlessly.

Debate Competitions

Speak up. Stand firm. Win the room. We teach students that their voice deserves to be heard.

Girls' Club Sessions

A safe circle. No judgment. Just honest conversations about rights, dreams, and the futures they are boldly building.

Where Hope Lives

One small library is quietly rewriting futures across Mathare

Behind a modest doorway in Mathare, children step out of chaos and into calm. Here, they find more than books — they find mentors who listen, a community that shows up, and the quiet, steady support that turns everyday struggle into a real shot at a different future.

Meet The Team

The People Turning Hope Into Action

Behind every child who discovers a love for reading, every young person who finds their confidence, and every program that creates lasting change — is a team that believes transformation starts with people.

Founder

David Odour

Founder & Programs Lead

"I didn't just learn to read in this library — I learned to think ahead, on the chessboard and in life. Now I get to open that same door for the next child."

David grew up right here in Mathare — and once sat where these students sit today. Now he leads the mission, turning chessboards and library shelves into launchpads for the next generation.

Kevin Olengo

Community Engagement & Partnerships

"Change doesn't happen to a community — it happens with one."

Kevin believes real change starts when communities work together — and he's the one building those bridges.

Damaris Adhiambo

Education & Child Development

"Every child deserves a room where they feel like they belong."

Damaris creates classrooms where every child feels safe enough to dream — and supported enough to try.

Nephine Nephine

Youth Empowerment Coordinator

"Your circumstances are not your future — I'm here to prove that."

Nephine helps young people find their voice — and the courage to lead with it.

David Kanguto

Operations & Community Programs

"Impact isn't glamorous. It's showing up, every single day."

David keeps the wheels turning behind the scenes — turning big ideas into daily impact for the families who need it.

James Ochieng

Head Coach, BoardLords Chess Club

"A queen sacrificed is not a queen lost — it's a lesson the board just taught you."

James turns thirty-two black-and-white squares into a classroom — where every move a child makes builds the patience and foresight they'll need far beyond the game.

Every child deserves the chance to learn. Every young person deserves the chance to lead. Every community deserves hope. Together with our volunteers, partners, and the families we serve — we're building that future, one learner at a time.
