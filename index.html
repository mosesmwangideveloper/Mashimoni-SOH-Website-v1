YouthRiseChangeMakers | Mashimoni School of Hope

Empowering Mathare Through Education & Opportunity

In the heart of Mathare, one of Nairobi's largest informal settlements, we run a School of Hope — a safe learning space, evening library, and youth empowerment hub for children and teens facing poverty, violence, and educational inequality.
A home base. Built by the community, for the community — where students who study evenings and weekends, who are first-generation learners, who work by day to survive, finally have a room that's theirs.
