About Us

Getting to know the people, the purpose, and the story behind YouthRiseChangeMakers.

Who We Are

A Community Raising Its Own

YouthRiseChangeMakers (Mashimoni School of Hope) is a grassroots, community-driven organization in Mathare, Nairobi. Founded with a simple belief — that every child deserves equal access to learning and opportunities — we provide safe spaces, mentorship, and resources that empower young people to thrive.

We exist to break the cycle of poverty by equipping children with knowledge, resilience, and the tools to succeed both inside and outside the classroom.

Our work is shaped by the voices of the community, because real change begins when families, teachers, and neighbors join hands for the sake of the next generation.

Mission

To ensure every child in Mathare has access to quality education, mentorship, and holistic support — equipping them with skills and opportunities to succeed in life.

Vision

A future where every child in Mathare succeeds — whether through education, sports, or creative expression — and becomes a confident leader driving positive change in their community.

Our History

YouthRiseChangeMakers (Mashimoni School of Hope) was born out of a simple observation: too many children in Mathare were missing school due to financial challenges, lack of safe learning spaces, and limited opportunities for growth. What began as a small community library where children could read, study, and dream has grown into a vibrant center of hope — a lifeline for families that combines education, mentorship, and extracurricular activities.

Over the years, we've witnessed firsthand how consistent support and care can transform young lives. Through the generous partnership of organizations such as ChallengeAid and Education in a Suitcase, as well as the digital learning platform Tutor-Web, our learners have been able to thrive academically and personally. These collaborations have provided access to digital lessons, mentorship, and essential resources that continue to nurture both academic and personal development.

Beyond education, these partnerships have made a tangible difference in daily life — from weekly shopping distributions and sanitary towels for girls, to programs like the Girls and Boys Club, Mathare Got Talent, and engaging Zoom lessons that keep learning alive even outside the classroom.

Today, YouthRiseChangeMakers (Mashimoni School of Hope) proudly serves hundreds of children and families. Together with our partners and supporters, we continue to prove that when a community invests in its young people — giving them the tools, guidance, and opportunities they deserve — transformation is not just possible, it's inevitable.

I didn't just learn to read in this library — I learned to think ahead, on the chessboard and in life. Now I get to open that same door for the next child.

David Oduor — Founder & Programs Lead, once a student at Mashimoni School of Hope
