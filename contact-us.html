Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?

You can donate through the Donate button at the top of any page, or reach out to us directly and we'll walk you through the options.

How can I volunteer?

Send us a message using the form above and select "Volunteer" — tell us your skills and availability and our team will follow up with next steps.

How are donated funds used?

Funds go directly toward our programmes — the library, chess training, e-learning sessions, and essential support for children like sanitary towels and school supplies.

Can I visit Mashimoni School of Hope in person?

Yes. Reach out beforehand so we can arrange a good time — our opening hours are listed above.
