Our Programmes

We're building brighter futures for children, youth, and families in Mathare.

From a quiet library corner to a chessboard mid-match, every programme is a door we keep open for the children, youth, and families of Mathare.

Education

Evening Library & Learning Hub

Some children walk through a door and find a library. Ours walk through a door and find belonging. Warm light, quiet corners, shelves stacked with worlds waiting to be opened — every evening, this room turns a hard day into a fresh start. No book stays unread here. No child sits unseen.

Open daily All ages - children, teens & adults Mashimoni Library
Community

Group Discussions

Give a child a topic, and watch them find their voice. In every circle, opinions are welcomed, not corrected. Kids learn to disagree with respect, listen without interrupting, and speak up like their ideas matter — because they do. This is where confidence gets its first rehearsal.

Held daily All ages Mashimoni Library
Education

E-learning Sessions

A laptop. A signal. And suddenly, the whole world is in the room. Through live e-learning sessions, students in Mathare sit shoulder-to-shoulder — virtually — with educators who believe geography should never decide how far a child can go.

During school holidays All ages Mashimoni Library
Chess

Chess Training & Competitions

Every match starts the same way: one pawn, one decision. But what a child learns on this board — patience, focus, the courage to think three moves ahead — follows them long after the game ends. Here, Mathare's next grandmasters are already making their opening moves.

Wednesdays & Fridays All ages Mashimoni Library
Debate

Debate Competitions

Give them a microphone, and they'll give you a reason to believe. In every debate, students learn to build an argument, hold their ground, and win a room with nothing but words and conviction. This is where young voices become impossible to ignore.

Scheduled through the term All ages Venue varies
Girls' Empowerment

Girls' Club Sessions

A closed door. An open conversation. In this circle, girls talk about the things that matter most — their bodies, their boundaries, their futures — without shame and without judgment. Here, every girl leaves a little braver than she arrived.

Weekly Girls, all ages Mashimoni Library

I didn't just learn to read in this library — I learned to think ahead, on the chessboard and in life. Now I get to open that same door for the next child.

David Oduor — Founder & Programs Lead, once a student at Mashimoni School of Hope
