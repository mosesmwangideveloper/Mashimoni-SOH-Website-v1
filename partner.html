How it Works

The Partnering Process

Simple, personal, and built on trust — here's what happens after you reach out.

1

Reach Out

Send us a message telling us about your organisation and how you'd like to partner.

2

Meet The Team

Talk directly with David Oduor, our Founder & Programs Lead, to discuss goals and fit.

3

Define The Partnership

Together we agree on what support looks like — funding, mentorship, or in-kind resources.

4

Start Making Impact

We onboard you into our programmes and keep you updated on the difference you're making.
